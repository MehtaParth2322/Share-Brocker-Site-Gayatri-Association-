Gains in HDFC twins and IT stocks helped BSE Sensex stage a 280- point recovery on Tuesday, even as the index closed marginally lower..

Nifty formed a Dragonfly Doji on the daily scale, as it recovered nearly 80 points from itsintraday low in the last hour of day. The index is hovering near the 11,550 – 11,750 zone from past six sessions and some follow-up buying could take it towards a new lifetime high of 11,850 and 12,000 levels while on the downside support is seen at 11,666 - MOFSL


Indian stock market didn’t mimic the US market and opened on a weak note. Nifty50 faced strong resistance at the 11,757 level. Nifty Media, IT, Metal and Financial Services ended in the green, while Nifty PSU bank was the biggest loser with a 3.3% fall. Nifty IT has given a new breakout, and therefore, there is massive scope in the mid-cap IT counters - Samco Securities


We expect markets to remain volatile and rangebound in the near term given earnings outcome and central election progress. On the global front, movement in crude oil prices and currency would be closely monitored by the market participants. Further, auto sales numbers for April and outcome of FOMC meet (on May 1) will remain on market’s radar. Investor focus should remain on fundamentally sound companies with strong growth prospects. Meanwhile, traders should avoid risky leveraged positions - Religare Broking
